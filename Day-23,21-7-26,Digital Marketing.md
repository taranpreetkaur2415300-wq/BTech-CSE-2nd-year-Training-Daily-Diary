# LinkdIn

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform jithe students, job seekers, professionals te businesses apni professional profile bana sakde han. Is platform te users jobs search kar sakde han, companies follow kar sakde han, apne skills showcase kar sakde han, te professional connections bana sakde han.

### Main Features of LinkedIn
#### Professional Profile
#### Job Search
#### Networking
#### Posts & Articles
#### Company Pages
#### Messaging
#### Skill Endorsements
#### Recommendations
#### Learning Courses (LinkedIn Learning)

## 1. LinkedIn Profile

A LinkedIn Profile is an online professional resume that highlights a user's education, experience, skills, and achievements.

## 2. LinkedIn Jobs

The LinkedIn Jobs section helps users find job opportunities based on their skills, experience, and interests.

## 3. LinkedIn Pictures (Images)

LinkedIn allows users to upload images to make posts more engaging and visually appealing.

## 4. LinkedIn Articles

LinkedIn Articles allow users to publish detailed, long-form professional content.

## LinkedIn Post
A LinkedIn Post is a short piece of content shared on LinkedIn to communicate ideas, updates, achievements, knowledge, or professional experiences with your network. It appears in the LinkedIn news feed and helps users engage with others through likes, comments, reposts, and shares. Individuals, companies, and organizations use LinkedIn posts to build their professional presence, increase visibility, and connect with a wider audience.
