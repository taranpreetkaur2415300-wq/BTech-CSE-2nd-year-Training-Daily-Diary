# Alsoasked.com(Website) 
AlsoAsked is a keyword research tool that collects and visualizes Google's "People Also Ask" questions, helping users find related 
search queries, understand user intent, and create SEO-friendly content.

## Semrush 
SEMrush is an all-in-one digital marketing and SEO tool used to improve a website's visibility on search engines. It helps users 
perform keyword research, competitor analysis, site audits, backlink analysis, rank tracking, and content optimization to increase 
organic traffic and online performance.
