# Twitter(X)
X (formerly known as Twitter) is a social media platform that allows users to share short text posts, images, videos, GIFs, and links. It is widely used for news, discussions, marketing, networking, and real-time updates. Users can follow other accounts, interact with posts, and participate in trending conversations.

### Features of Twitter (X)
#### Create and share posts (tweets)
#### Follow people and organizations
#### Like, Reply, Repost (Retweet), and Quote posts
#### Upload images, videos, GIFs, and polls


## Twitter (X) Post (Tweet)

A Tweet (also called a Post on X) is a message shared on the platform. Users can post text, images, videos, GIFs, polls, or links to share information, opinions, or updates.

### Features of a Tweet
##### Add text, images, videos, GIFs, or links
#### Use hashtags (#) for better reach
##### Mention users with @username
#### Receive likes, replies, reposts, quotes, and bookmarks
#### View impressions and engagement (for eligible accounts)
