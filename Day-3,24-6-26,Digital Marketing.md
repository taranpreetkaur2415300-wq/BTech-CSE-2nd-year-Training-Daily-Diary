# Schema Markup Generator and Schema Validator

## For Product Schema

### Product schema Markup Generator

1. Search "Schema Markup Generator" on google.
2. Select Product, fill in the details, and generate the markup.

<img width="403" height="317" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9eafdb19-a1cc-4bb8-8418-481f45cfd248" />

### Product Markup Validator

1. Enter the product page URL or paste the product schema code into the product Markup validator.
2. Click Validate to check for any errors or warnings in the markup.
