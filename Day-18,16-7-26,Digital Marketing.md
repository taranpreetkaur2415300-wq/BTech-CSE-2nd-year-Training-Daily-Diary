search query.
### Why is Keyword Ranking Important?

##### 1.Increases website visibility.
##### 2.Helps attract more organic traffic.
##### 3.Measures SEO performance.
##### 4.Identifies which keywords need improvement.

Facebook is a social media platform that enables users to communicate, share content, build communities, and promote businesses online.

## Facebook Page Creation

A Facebook Page is a public profile created for a business, brand, organization, public figure, or community. It helps you promote your services, share updates, interact with customers, and build an online presence.
### Steps to Create a Facebook Page

#### 1.Log in to your Facebook account.
