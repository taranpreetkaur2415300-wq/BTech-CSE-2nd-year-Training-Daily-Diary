# Social Book Marketing
Social Book Marketing is a free online business directory and social networking platform where businesses can create a profile to improve their online presence, SEO, and visibility.
## What is Social Book Marketing?
It allows businesses to:
#### 1.Create a free business listing.
#### 2.Add business details (name, address, phone number, website, email, etc.).
#### 3.Share products, services, photos, and updates.
#### 4.Get backlinks to their website, which can help with search engine optimization (SEO).
#### 5.Connect with potential customers.

# Directory Submission Sites
Directory submission is an off-page SEO technique where a business website is submitted to online business directories. It helps improve website visibility, create backlinks, increase domain authority, and make it easier for customers to find the business online.

## Steps

### 1. Visit the directory submission website and click Add Business, Submit Listing, or Register.
