# Article Prompt ( Non Promotional)
Article Prompt (Non-Promotional) means an instruction given to an AI or writer to create an article that is informative and
educational, without promoting any product, service, brand, or company. The main purpose is to help readers understand a topic 
and answer their questions in simple, clear language.
