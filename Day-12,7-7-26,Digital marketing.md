# Promotional vs. Non-Promotional Content

## Promotional Content

1. Focuses directly on the company or brand.
2. Promotes products or services.
3. Encourages users to purchase or contact the business.
4. Uses marketing-oriented language.

## Non-Promotional Content

1. Focuses on solving users' problems.
2. Educates and informs readers.
3. Avoids excessive brand promotion.
4. Mentions products or services only where they naturally fit.
5. Builds trust by providing valuable information.
