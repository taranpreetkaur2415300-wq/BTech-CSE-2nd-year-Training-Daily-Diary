# OFF Page
Off-page (or off-site) refers to all the actions and SEO techniques performed outside of your own website to boost its 
credibility, authority, and ranking
## Core Off-Page Strategies
### Link Building (Backlinks)
Acquiring hyperlinks from other authoritative and relevant websites back to yours. This passes "link equity" and signals trustworthiness.
### Digital PR
Earning organic mentions, interviews, or features in news outlets, magazines, and industry publications.
### Brand Mentions
Unlinked references to your brand across blogs, forums, or social media that increase awareness.
### Social Media Marketing
Promoting your content on platforms like LinkedIn, Reddit, or Facebook to drive referral traffic and increase discoverability.
### Local SEO (Citations & Reviews)
Building local visibility through online reviews (e.g., Google Business Profile) and directory listings.
