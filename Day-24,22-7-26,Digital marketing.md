# LinkedIn navigation tabs

## 1. Home

### The Home page is the main feed where users can

#### View posts from connections and companies.
#### Like, comment, and repost content.



## 2. My Network

### The My Network section helps users build and manage professional connections.

### Features
#### Accept and send connection requests.
#### View suggested connections.
#### Follow people and companies.


## 3. Jobs

### The Jobs section helps users search and apply for job opportunities.

### Features
#### Search jobs by title or keyword.
#### Filter jobs by location, company, and experience level.
#### Save jobs.


## 4. Messaging

### The Messaging section allows users to communicate with their professional network.

### Features

#### Send and receive messages.
#### Share files, links, and documents.

## 5. Notifications

### The Notifications section keeps users updated about activities related to their LinkedIn account.

### Features


#### Likes, comments, and reposts on your posts.
#### Profile views and mentions.
