# Canva
## Introduction
Canva is a user-friendly online graphic design platform that allows anyone to create professional-looking designs without advanced design skills. It offers a simple drag-and-drop interface, making it easy to design presentations, posters, social media posts, resumes, flyers, videos, logos, and much more.

<img width="817" height="392" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1d57a0e9-61f1-40fe-8270-5694acd170ac" />

## Key Features of Canva
#### Drag-and-drop editor 
Easily add and arrange text, images, icons, and shapes.
#### Thousands of templates 
Ready-made templates for presentations, posters, invitations, certificates, and social media graphics.
#### Stock resources
Access millions of photos, videos, illustrations, and audio files.
#### Collaboration 
Work with teammates in real time and share designs instantly.
#### AI-powered tools 
Generate images, remove backgrounds, create text, and edit designs using AI features.
#### Cloud storage 
Save designs online and access them from any device.
